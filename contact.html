Common Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked questions

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

Our emergency response team aims to arrive at your location within 30 minutes for urgent calls. We operate 24/7/365 and are always ready to help.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all projects. Contact us to schedule an appointment and get a detailed quote.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, all major credit cards, and debit cards. We also offer financing options for larger projects through our approved partners.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Absolutely! All our plumbers are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We maintain comprehensive insurance coverage and all necessary certifications.

Can I schedule an appointment online?

Yes! You can fill out our contact form above, or call us directly to schedule an appointment. We typically offer same-day or next-day service.

Common Questions

Do I need to be home?

Yes, someone 18+ must be present during the service. We'll confirm your availability when scheduling.

What should I prepare?

Clear access to the problem area, remove any obstacles, and have your ID ready for verification.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes, with at least 24 hours notice for scheduled appointments. Emergency calls can be modified anytime.

Payment timing?

Payment is due upon completion of work. We accept cash, cards, checks, and offer financing options.
